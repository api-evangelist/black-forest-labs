Black Forest Labs partnered with ASUS and NVIDIA to bring FLUX.2 [klein], a 4B image generation model, preloaded on ASUS ProArt laptops with RTX GPUs, enabling sub-5-second image generation on an 8GB VRAM laptop without internet or API calls. It marks the first FLUX model to ship on consumer hardware.
