Black Forest Labs CEO Robin Rombach addressed G7 leaders about the importance of open innovation in AI development, arguing that visual AI models should be made openly available while maintaining responsible safeguards against misuse. He emphasized that open technology is essential for competition and transparency, balanced with safety measures against harms like deepfakes.
